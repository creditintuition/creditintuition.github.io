About

I build quantitative risk in lending from first principles — and I arrived at it by an unusual route. I began as a fixed-income quant, pricing bonds, CDS, prepayment and interest-rate risk with stochastic calculus and Monte Carlo; spent years in e-commerce analytics scaling online marketplaces; and brought both to fintech, where I have led risk and data science across a decade of lending.

The work has been deliberately cross-functional. I have owned credit, collections, RCU and fraud, treasury and ECL, and data science end to end — as CRO-equivalent at Cars24 and Turno, Chief Analytics Officer at Lendingkart, MSME business head at Protium and KreditBee, and founder of a securitisation-and-servicer venture — building and running teams of fifty to a hundred and fifty.

The constant is a quantitative lens on lending: transition-matrix ECL, alternative-data underwriting on telecom, telematics and battery state-of-health, PTC stress-testing, and behavioural scorecards — deriving the answer rather than importing a template.
